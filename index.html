  • Bio
    • Owen Lantz is a Grammy-winning engineer, producer, and songwriter. Originally from Portland, Owen has years of experience working with high profile clients under his belt, all while simultaneously developing upcoming artists and helping them achieve their creative & sonic goals. Owen is a cofounder of Maplewood Studios, Collectively Good, and ScriptFlip Digital.
